About Us

Embark on a journey with us through our rich history, steeped in a decade of successful early childhood development in Woodstock. Betsey Educare's roots trace back to a thriving center diligently overseen by our late mother-in-law, Louise Elizabeth Bruce. Her legacy endures as the baton was gracefully passed to Stephanie Esau, her daughter-in-law. Now based in Cape Town's CBD area, we proudly serve children from diverse backgrounds, ensuring they receive the best start in their educational journey.
Stephanie, equipped and inspired by her predecessor, envisions a vibrant Early Childhood Development (ECD) center that grants high-quality education, a supportive environment, and exemplary service. At Betsey Educare, we are driven by a passion to create a nurturing space where children can flourish. Our mission is to provide a fun, spacious learning environment that encourages exploration, authenticity, and the realization of their fullest potential.

Our Services


Daycare Services

We provide Early Childhood Development services for children aged 3 months to 5 years. Following the South African government's CAPS and National Curriculum Framework with elements of Montessori principles, our unique approach incorporates visual, auditory, and kinesthetic learning in each activity. This interactive, fun learning style helps children grasp concepts faster and prepares them thoroughly for big school.

School holiday Programs and After-School Care

Our after-school care and school holiday programs provide safe, enriching environments for children to learn, grow, and explore new interests beyond regular school hours and during breaks. Activities include creative arts, sports, and educational workshops, all supervised by dedicated staff to ensure children are engaged, safe, and making lasting memories.
